Who We Are

We were founded by chance during the summer of 2018. Bill Johnson, the owner and CEO, developed an addiction purchasing items at garage sales. Being faced with the choice of getting rid of these items or find happiness face a divorce, he chose the former and began selling items to friends. This quickly blossomed into a full fledged thrift store.


Community Support

Bill believes in community. Therefore, 50 percent of ALL proceeds are placed back into the local community. This has taken the form of donations to combat hunger, purchase of school supplies for needy children, and purchase of books donated to the local library.

A purchase from us is an investment in your community!
