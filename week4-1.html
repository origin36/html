Craftsman Lawn Tractor

Just Arrived!!! Craftsman 46 in. W 725 cc Lawn Tractor Riding. Model Number 27065. This Craftsman Pro Series garden tractor is perfect for large lawns and heavy-duty hauling. Stay on top of weekly maintenance with the tap of the Craftsman Smart Lawn app on your smartphone.

Description

With 12 different cutting height positions and Consistent-Cut™ technology, you'll be able to power through any yard, even yards with uneven terrain and tall grass. Track run time, order replacements, get service notifications and watch troubleshooting videos. It's never been easier to care for your lawn and your mower. Ideal for medium and large lawns with any type of terrain.

Craftsman Smart Lawn connects your tractor to your smart phone via Bluetooth and gives you maintenance information. Garden tractors are designed for heavier yard work. Powered by a 26 HP 747cc Kohler 7000 Elite V-Twin engine to handle the tough jobs.

Foot pedal hydrostatic transmission gives you a smooth ride without having to shift gears. Consistent-Cut™ technology changes the engine power for an even, precise cut every time. The platinum spark plug starts up the engine with ease. A 50" wide deck cuts a large path of grass in a single pass to finish in less time. Spring-assisted lift system features a total of 12 different cutting height positions. A deck wash system connects to your hose to clean out debris underneath. 3-in-1 deck design lets you bag, mulch or discharge your clippings. Runs up to 5.5 MPH to get through bigger yards faster!
