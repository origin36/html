Our Story

We were founded by chance during the summer of 2018. Bill Johnson, the owner and CEO, developed an addiction purchasing items at garage sales. As the his collection grew, he contemplated opening up a store. He begain selling online and quickly earned the capital to open a local store, Resale City.

Community

Bill believes in community. Therefore, 50 percent of ALL proceeds are placed back into the local community.This has taken the form of donations to combat hunger,purchase of school supplies for needy children, and purchase of books donated tothe local library. A purchase from us is an investment in your community!
